Artist: Hand Drawn Euclidean Geometry-Influenced Artwork

In 2015 I started creating hand-drawn geometry artwork, which was to be for the purpose of creating artwork for my Techno/House digital record label 'Fraktion Music'.

I worked through Euclid's postulates in sequence, where I understood the theory, then I elaborated on this encompassing my own concepts taken from the music.

Similarly to coding, this type of fine arts takes an amost scientific-level of precision, sometime where I'm judging close to 1/10 of a millimeter by eye, and there are times where it's essential to pause and step back to look at the bigger picture again, to remember what's going on at the higher level. Not to mention, this type of work can be painstaking and requires a high level of patience, but however painstaking it may get, I never give up until the work is finished, and I find that extremely rewarding.

I am in current process of bringing this work back to life, and redocumenting it, for now you can see my old blog here.
