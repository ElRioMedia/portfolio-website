Fine Arts, Euclidean Geometry-Influenced, Hand-Drawn

I'm passionate about art and have created my own style of geometric artwork derived from combining studyies of Euclid's postulates with musical concepts, often including spiritual notions.

The pieces are hand drawn using no more than a pencil, pen, compass, protractor and a ruler. Then they're scanned in and digitally processed.

Similarly to coding, this requires a great deal of patience, focus and attention to detail, and while sometimes can be a painstaking process, can also be extremely rewarding when you finally crack it.

Catering and Hospitality: Classically Trained Fine Dining Chef

Since starting work in kitchens in 2002, I ended up as a highly trained and qualified chef, cooking technical fine dining cuisine up to a 3 rosette standard.

My work in kitchens is where I developed my team working skills, management skills, delegation, leadership, communication and loyalty.
