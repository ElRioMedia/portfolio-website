Director, Business Development

As good as this was when it worked and flowed, I did hit a wall with my technical skills as and wanted to move up from using drag and drop page builders (Wix / WordPress) to studying to become a fully-fledged Web Developer.

Artist: Hand Drawn Euclidean Geometry-Influenced Artwork

In 2015 I started creating hand-drawn geometry artwork, which was for the purpose of designing album artwork for my Techno-House record label 'Fraktion Music'.

I worked through Euclid's postulates in sequence, understanding his theories, which I then elaborated on encompassing my own concepts influenced by music.

Similarly to coding... drawing a comparison to web development, other than it's visual aesthetics, this type of fine arts takes an almost scientific-level of precision, sometime where I'm judging close to 1/10 of a millimeter by eye. It can be a painstaking process and requires a certain level of patience. There can are times when you get too drawn in and lose focus, which requires you to take a step back and look at the bigger picture, and to reiterate what's going on at the higher level. Not to mention, this type of work can be painstaking and requires a high level of patience, but however painstaking it may get, I never give up until the work is finished, and I find that extremely rewarding.

I am in current process of bringing this work back to life, and re-documenting it, for now you can see my old blog here.
